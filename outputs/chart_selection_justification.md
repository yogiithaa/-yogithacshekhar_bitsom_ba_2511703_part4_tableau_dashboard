# Chart Selection Justification

## Sales Trend View

### Business Question

How are sales changing over time?

### Chart Type

Line Chart

### Why This Chart Was Selected

Line charts are the most effective visualization for identifying trends, seasonality, and fluctuations over time.

### Fields Used

* X-Axis: Order Date (Month)
* Y-Axis: Sales
* Color: Region

### Design Principle Applied

Trend visualization and temporal analysis.

### Mistake Avoided

Avoided using pie charts or bar charts for time-series analysis.

---

## Regional Performance View

### Business Question

Which region generates the highest sales and profit?

### Chart Type

Horizontal Bar Chart

### Why This Chart Was Selected

Bar charts enable accurate comparison of performance across geographic regions.

### Fields Used

* Rows: Region
* Columns: Sales
* Color: Profit
* Label: Profit

### Design Principle Applied

Ranking and comparison.

### Mistake Avoided

Avoided 3D charts and unnecessary visual effects.

## Dashboard KPI Cards

### Business Question

What are the most important business metrics?

### Chart Type

KPI Cards

### Why This Chart Was Selected

Executive users require immediate visibility into critical business metrics.

### Fields Used

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin
* Return Rate

### Design Principle Applied

Information hierarchy and executive summarization.

### Mistake Avoided

Avoided requiring users to derive key metrics from detailed charts.
